A warm welcome to my webpage! I created this webpage specifically for the module "GEOG5995M - Programming for Social Science: Core Skills".
I am a PhD student at the University of Leeds. The main purpose of my research is to use agent-based modelling in order to simulate environmental, behavioural, economic and social determinants of obesity.

The first assignment required me to create an Agent-based Model (ABM) using the Python programming language. In the ABM sheeps are illustrated inside the environment and they perform various tasks such as eating the grass. Further details about this Agent-based Model are available on my GitHub.
